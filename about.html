The Cast

Crystal Clark

Crystal Clark is a graduate of the Second City's A-E Improvisation Program and will complete the Sketch Writing Program this summer. Her writing class is producing a show that will run at the Second City for four weeks beginning in late June. She has been a member of the the improv group Very Bendy since June 2014. Crystal joined the Army National Guard at the age of 19, serving two deployments to Iraq in 2004 and 2008. During her eight years of service, she worked as a logistician, broadcast journalist, and one tour as an armed escort.
Follower her on Twitter @thecrystallion

Tyler Decker

Tyler Decker is a graduate of the Second City's A-E Improvisation Program and Sketch Writing Programs. His Second City Writing Six class produced the show "Black Sheep Make Better Sweaters". He is currently working on the Annoyance Improvisation Program. Tyler enlisted in the United States Army in August 2001. His Military Occupation Specialty was Explosive Ordnance Disposal. He served two deployments to Iraq in 2003 and 2005. While in Iraq his team disposed of over 80 Improvised Explosive Devices, and assisted in the destruction of over 100k pounds of munitions.

Cam Dupre

Hailing from Baton Rouge, Louisiana Cam Dupre is a proud southerner who got lost in the woods and ended up in Chicago in 2012. Cam is a graduate of iO, The Annoyance, Second City's Music Conservatory programs and is currently a student in the Conservatory. Cam has performing on the Annoyance Mainstage (Fur Elites) as well as Second City's DeMaat Theater (Black Sheep Make Better Sweaters) and Donny's Skybox (Shaken Not Heard). He joined the Marine Corps at 18 right after high school. He deployed to Iraq's Al Anbar Province in 2008 and Afghanistan's Helmand province in 2009-2010 as part of 2d Battalion, 2d Marines Regiment Easy Company. Naval Unit Citation, Presidential Unit Citation, Overseas Service Medal (in lieu of 2nd Award), and Combat Action Ribbon are among his 9 totals awards.

Landis Ferderick

Landis Frederick is a graduate of the Second City A-E Improvisation Program. He is a performer with his independent improvisation/sketch group "Something To Put Down". Landis has performed on Second City's: "Coached Ensembles" with his team Sausage Inquisition. He is currently studying improvisation at IO. Landis is a Naval Reservist of seven years, and has served overseas in Balad, Iraq in 2010. He is a Construction Electrician serving in the Naval Construction Battalion- The Seabees. He has served in Logistics Support Operations in Pearl Harbor, Hawaii in 2012. He has also performed as a Cargo Handling Specialist in Manama, Bahrain in 2013.
